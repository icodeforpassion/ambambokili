About Ambambo Kili

Malayalam kids songs, stories, and Kerala cartoons crafted with love.

Ambambo Kili takes children on a joyful journey through the culture of Kerala. Our team of musicians, storytellers, and illustrators creates original Malayalam songs, bedtime tales, and imaginative cartoons that celebrate family values, nature, and curiosity. Each episode is crafted to be inclusive, singable, and educational while keeping the vibe of a friendly Kerala courtyard.

We produce every video with clean Malayalam diction, subtitles for early readers, and playful motion graphics. Families across the world tune in to bond over the folk rhythms, dance movements, and characters who feel like cousins from their own village. We are committed to safe, ad-friendly entertainment that parents can trust for preschoolers and primary school kids alike.

Contact us: hello@ambambokili.example. Collaborations, school programs, and feedback are always welcome.
